Known Bugs and Limitations
=========================

As of July 2025, the following known bugs and limitations exist in the DOORDARSHAN (OTT Platform) project:

1. No known critical bugs have been reported.
2. S3 upload failures (e.g., due to network or permission issues) may not always provide detailed error messages to the user.
3. Video streaming performance may vary depending on the user's network speed and S3 region.
4. Email delivery for password reset may be delayed or blocked by some email providers.
5. Admin actions (add/edit/delete) may not always roll back all changes if an error occurs mid-operation.
6. No support for video transcoding or format conversion (uploads must be in supported formats).
7. No automated tests for S3 integration or email delivery.
